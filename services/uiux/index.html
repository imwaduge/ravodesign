Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a UI/UX project take?

This depends on the project's complexity. A simple landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a full mobile app with 10+ screens could take 4-8 weeks, including research and revisions.

Do you also code (develop) the app or website?

This service is for **design only**. We provide 'developer-ready' design files (e.g., in Figma) that your developer can use to build the final product. We do not provide HTML, CSS, or programming services in this package.

What files will I receive upon completion?

You will receive a link to the complete, organized source file (usually Figma or Adobe XD), a UI Style Guide, and all necessary /assets (icons, images) exported for development.

What if I don't like the design?

We work collaboratively. We start with wireframes to agree on the structure before any color is added. Your package includes revision rounds, and we will refine the design based on your feedback until you are happy with the direction.
